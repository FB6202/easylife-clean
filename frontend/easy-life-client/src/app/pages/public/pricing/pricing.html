Frequently asked questions

help

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — cancel anytime with no questions asked. Your data stays accessible until the end of your billing period.

warning

What happens when I hit the Free limit?

You'll be notified when approaching limits. Existing data stays safe — you just won't be able to add more until you upgrade.

lock

Is my data safe?

All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We store everything on AWS with regular backups and 99.9% uptime.

swap_vert

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely — upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades at the end of your billing cycle.
